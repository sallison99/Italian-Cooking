                Pizza Sauce

                Ingredients

                12 oz whole tomatoes
                1 clove crushed garlic
                Tsp sugar
                Tsp crushed red pepper
                1/2 Tsp fennel seeds
                Salt and Pepper to taste 

                Directions:
                Puree whole tomatoes, add all ingredients and simmer until sauce thickens.
