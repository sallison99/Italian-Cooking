Pizza Sauce

Ingredients

                12 oz whole tomatoes
                1 clove crushed garlic
                1 onion sliced small and sauteed
                Tsp sugar
                Tsp crushed red pepper
                1/2 Tsp fennel seeds
                Salt and Pepper to taste

Directions

                Puree whole tomatoes, add all ingredients and 
                simmer until sauce thickens.

Pizza Dough

Ingredients

              1 1/2 Tablespoon yeast
              1 Tablespoon sugar
              1 1/2 cup water (100-110 degrees)
              1 1/2 tsp Salt
              2 Tablespoon olive oil
              2 Tablespoon honey
              4 cups flour

Directions

  1. Mix yeast sugare and water, cover until yeast bubbles and foams
  2. Add salt,olive oil and honey. Mix with whisk
  3. Stir in first 2 cups of flour and continue to add in flour 1/2 cup at a time until dough is soft
  4. Cover with a towel for at least 10 minutes.
  5. Flour a surface and roll out pizza dough.
  6. Cover with your favorite topppings and bake at 400 degress until pizza is crispy
